Why take the Lüscher-Color-Diagnostic®

– Non-verbal- Quickly and accurately measure your emotions through a simple choice of colors.

– Offer you a picture of the inner mood and unconscious emotions that determine your behaviour.

– A 75+ year unique methodology taught in universities worldwide USA, South America, Russian, Western and European, Japan, Australia. Translated into 9 languages. Culturally unbiased diagnostic tool.

– The results are based on the choices of color’s combination. Not only on what colors you like or don’t like.

– Objectively measures subjective states of the person. It is not what you think or wish to be or wish to perceived.

– Most widely used psychosomatic personality evaluation in the world.

– It has been applied in numerous research projects and in medical and sociological dissertations.
